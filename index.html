"What is in the universe is in the body; what is in the body is in the universe" — Agathiyar

Siddha medicine, one of the ancient medical traditions of Tamil Nadu, is a traditional healthcare system that integrates the human body, nature, and lifestyle. Developed in harmony with Tamil culture for centuries, this wisdom plays a vital role in maintaining health and promoting a balanced, natural lifestyle.

Basic Principles of Siddha

According to the Siddha medical tradition, the normal functions of the human body are governed by the balance of three fundamental physiological humors: Vadham (Air/Ether), Pittham (Fire), and Kabham (Earth/Water). Diet, lifestyle, and traditional medicine methods are used to support this balance.

Traditional Knowledge of Siddhars

The Tamil Siddhars developed various medical texts and formulation systems based on their profound scientific studies of nature, herbs, and human physiology. That traditional knowledge remains the main foundation of Siddha medicine to this day.

Our Pharmacy & Legacy

Established to honor the heritage of the Thanjavur medical tradition, Pandit Anandam Siddha & Ayurveda Marunthagam has been providing high-quality Siddha and Ayurvedic medicines to the residents of Rameswaram and Ramanathapuram districts for over 15 years.

Operating under the guidance of Mr. K. A. Nallathambi, our pharmacy is an official distributor of IMPCOPS (Indian Medical Practitioners' Co-operative Pharmacy and Stores Ltd.), ensuring that authentic, standard-grade Siddha and Ayurvedic medicines reach the public reliably.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve the values of traditional Siddha and Ayurvedic medical heritage, improving public health and well-being through standard-grade preparations and compassionate, reliable services.
